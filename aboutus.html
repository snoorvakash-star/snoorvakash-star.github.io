Our Center

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is set in acres of land in the heart of the majestic Argyll hills.
On our doorstep is not only magnificent scenery,
but also a breath-taking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities.
With activities designed to meet the needs of all ages and experiences of young people,
Lochquarry truly brings adventure to everyone.
Activities on offer at Lochquarry include:
Land Based(Hillwalking,Archery,Orienteering,Axe throwing)
Water Based(Kayaking,Canoeing,Powerboating)
Rope Based(Climbing,Abseiling,Pole climbing)

Our Staff
